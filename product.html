Our Vision

To meet the needs of our target customer base, we are developing a retrofit product that will automate your existing blinds and enable them with smart home features.

Product Features


  • Easy installation process to retrofit existing blinds with smart features

  • Direct control using mobile app or automated control using preset scheduling routines

  • Ability to save energy costs by automating opening and closing of blinds according to expected sunlight patterns

  • Voice control using Amazon Alexa, Google Home or Apple Siri assistant platforms

  • Connectivity to devices using WiFi and Bluetooth Low Energy interactions
