Our mission

At IO Labs, our long-term goal is to build smart home solutions that will help simplify boring daily tasks, so you can focus on the exciting things.

Our focus is on building usable, affordable and fun products!



Our product vision

We are currently working hard on developing user friendly and affordable solutions for automating residential window coverings.

Stay tuned for more details!



Our story

Meeting first as university classmates, then crossing paths again as colleagues in their first post-graduation jobs, our founders have developed extensive industry experience in the technology and manufacturing industries.

Through their joint interest in technology and innovation, they have decided to combine their experiences and skills to help deliver usable and fun products that will help everyday life.

Their past work experiences in user interface design, software architecture, hardware development, product management and data analytics will help us to bring relevant and cost-effective product features to our valued customers.
